Euro Step
Drive hard, plant one foot and step laterally to avoid the defender, finish with the other hand.
Key: sell the first step hard · Used by: Giannis, Harden
Finishing
Floater
A high-arcing one or two-hand shot released early in the lane to get it over shot blockers.
Key: soft touch, quick release · Used by: Trae Young, CP3
Finishing
Reverse Layup
Go under the basket and finish on the far side of the backboard, using the rim as a shield.
Key: use the far hand, attack baseline · Used by: Kyrie, SGA
Finishing
Up-and-Under
Fake a shot to get the defender airborne, then step through and finish underneath.
Key: make the shot fake believable · Used by: bigs and crafty guards
Finishing
Scoop Layup
A one-hand underhand scoop that creates high arc, useful against taller defenders in the lane.
Key: extend arm fully, high arc · Used by: Tony Parker, Kyrie
Finishing
Power Layup
Two-foot jump into contact, absorbing the foul while finishing strong through the defender.
Key: jump into the defender, protect the ball · Used by: LeBron, Zion

Key: hard attack first to sell the drive · Used by: Harden, Luka

Key: sell the pause with your eyes and shoulders · Used by: SGA, Kyrie

Key: the sell is in the wrist flick · Used by: Steph, Kyrie

Behind-the-Back
Wrap the ball behind your back to switch hands in tight traffic — a deceptive dribble with momentum.
Key: keep it low and tight · Used by: Magic, Kyrie, Rondo
1v1
Jab Step
From triple threat, jab a foot toward the defender to read their reaction — drive if they back up, shoot if they don't.
Key: read the defender's feet, not their eyes · Used by: KD, LeBron
1v1
Shot Fake
Lift the ball as if shooting to get the defender to jump, then drive or shoot after they land.
Key: bend your knees on the fake — don't just move your arms
1v1
Triple Threat
Caught position where you can shoot, pass, or drive — keeping the defender guessing at all times.
Key: stay low, protect the ball, keep the defender off-balance
1v1
Spin Move
Drive at the defender, plant your pivot foot and spin 180° away from them to open a new lane.
Key: keep the dribble low through the spin · Used by: LeBron, young Kobe
1v1
Between-the-Legs Cross
Dribble the ball through your legs to switch hands — tighter, more protected than a regular crossover in close quarters.
Key: stay low, don't telegraph with your head · Used by: Kyrie, Steph
1v1
Pull-Up Mid-Range
Attack off the dribble and stop suddenly to hit a jumper — the bread and butter shot in 1v1 settings.
Key: hard stop with bent knees · Used by: KD, Kawhi
1v1
Rocker Step
Jab toward the defender, rock back, then jab again — creates a rhythm that makes them bite on the second jab.
Key: be patient, let them react first · Used by: KD, Dirk
